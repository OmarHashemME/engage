## Common Idioms about Feelings

Idioms are often used to describe feelings and emotions in a more expressive way. Here are a few common ones.

### On cloud nine
- **Meaning:** To be extremely happy and joyful.
- **Example:** "When she got the job offer, she was **on cloud nine** for the rest of the day."

### Under the weather
- **Meaning:** To feel slightly ill or sick.
- **Example:** "I'm not coming to the party tonight, I'm feeling a bit **under the weather**."

### Butterflies in my stomach
- **Meaning:** To be very nervous or anxious, usually about something you are about to do.
- **Example:** "I always get **butterflies in my stomach** before I have to give a presentation."

Try to notice these idioms when you are listening to English. Can you think of a time you felt any of these ways?
